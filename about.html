On Reclaiming Human Agency

Digital spaces are essential to human collaboration, yet they have come to diminish our agency — our capacity to act independently and make free choices.

We envision tools that restore agency by enabling:

• Individuals to understand and improve their shared digital spaces
• Builders to create and monetize net-new capabilities
• Markets to incentivize positive-sum value creation
• Communities to achieve shared goals while honoring individual autonomy

This isn't just about better software.

It's about reclaiming human agency.

---

Our first step toward this vision is Cartographer — an open-source tool for agency discovery and collaboration management. It enables communities to:

• Understand how their systems and resources are actually used
• See how people and teams really work together
• Discover critical capabilities and dependencies
• Take action to achieve better outcomes

We've partnered with businesses, open source projects, and service providers to craft the first version of Cartographer, which is currently under active development.
